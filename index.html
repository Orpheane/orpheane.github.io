About Me

A creative mind
building things

Hello, I’m Orpheane.

Art has always been a part of my life, in many different forms. From music to photography, I have explored a wide range of styles over the years, driven by passion, perfectionism, and the desire to share the vast imaginative world that lives inside my mind.

Beyond creation itself, I have always cared about helping others in ways that truly fit them.

With the evolution of artificial intelligence and the tools now available to the public, and thanks also to my life partner, a passionate hardcore developer, I began learning new skills through vibe coding.

For me, every project starts as a blank page filled with endless possibilities.

Rift Studio Rework & Design

Pet Rift

Pet Rift was originally created almost four years ago by Rift Studio, a talented independent French team. For more than two years, they brought players original weekly updates.

At the end of December 2024, we - my life partner and I - took over the project with their full trust, driven by our passion and love for a game we had played ourselves. We reworked the entire game to improve its optimization. On my side, I also redesigned some existing areas, created new ones, and designed over 100 new pets for players to enjoy after the rework went live.

Rift Studio Game Dev

Rift Slasher

With Rift Slasher, we - my life partner and I - decided to build something new from the ground up, starting only with a few assets and pets generously provided by the studio, who continued to believe in us. Their support meant so much that we chose to publish the game under their name and studio.

The game found a small but meaningful audience, and visually, it remains the project I am most proud of.

AI Agent Coach

Emilie

Emilie is a ChatGPT agent designed as an emotional regulation and personal development coach: a human-like presence, without rigid methods.

She has been trained intensively for months and is now online, accessible to everyone. Emilie is currently available in French, with multilingual support planned for the near future.

Chrome Extension Productivity

Cozy Aquarium

Cozy Aquarium is my very first web extension, created to help people move through their daily tasks while enjoying a peaceful and comforting experience.

Through a gamification system I designed, the extension turns productivity into something softer, more motivating, and more enjoyable. It is my biggest one-woman project so far, and I hope to release it on Chrome by the end of July 2026.

Chrome Extension Music

Perfect Ear

Perfect Ear is a web extension made for people who love music as much as I do.

It is simple, functional, and works in real time with whatever you are listening to online. Like Cozy Aquarium, it is planned for release on Chrome by the end of July 2026.
